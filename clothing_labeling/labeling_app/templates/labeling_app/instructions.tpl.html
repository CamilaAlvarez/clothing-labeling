Instructions

You will be presented an image and a clothing category. You job is to use the cropper to wrap the garment in the picture.

To modify the cropper's size you must grab it by its corners

To move it around the image you must grab it by the center.

If you don't understand what garment the category referes to, you must only click the category name and descriptive image will be shown.

Once the item is correctly wrapped up, the only thing left is to press the Next button

If the category is not present in the image press the This is wrong! button. By doing this you will let us know that the image was labeled incorrectly.

After labeling 10 images you will be given a code, which is actually the answer to the questionnaire on Mechanical Turk.

If you press the Obtain more images!! button you will be assigned 10 more images. Once you finish labeling them all you will be given another code.

To list all the codes you have gained, press the Show codes button.
